About Us

The Organization aims to develop and empower youth through various industries and engage them within our community. This involves efforts to cultivate responsible youth dedicated to our country, promoting health and steering clear of extreme or questionable ideologies. It also entails establishing a platform to enhance and elevate youth skills, while increasing their engagement in various community activities.

Vision:

Upbringing generations of responsible youth.

Mission:

To become the platform for youth to grow, enjoy, and succeed.

Knot by YOUN: Knot aims to provide matrimonial services to our youth through Islamic values.

Our Services

  • Profile Development: Guidance to create a meaningful, faith-based profile.
  • Matchmaking: Personalized matchmaking services based on shared values and preferences.
  • Engagement Planning: Support in organizing an engagement that aligns with Islamic values.
  • Marriage Function Planning: Assistance in planning the marriage function, keeping it aligned with faith-based customs.
